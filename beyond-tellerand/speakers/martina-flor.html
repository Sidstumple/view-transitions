Martina Flor

The Art & Business of Lettering Design

Martina Flor is a lettering artist and type designer based in Berlin. She combines her talents as both a designer and an illustrator to create unique lettering and typography for clients around the world. She is the author of "The Golden Secrets of Lettering" and runs the online platform Lettering Design, where she teaches lettering and typography.
