Paula Zuccotti is a trend forecaster and ethnographer who specializes in understanding how people interact with objects and technology. She is the author of "The Future of Everything" and has worked with companies like Google, Samsung, and IKEA. Paula helps organizations understand emerging trends and design for the future.
