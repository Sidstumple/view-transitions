Paula will take us on a journey through the objects that shape our daily lives, exploring how design influences our behavior and emotions. She'll share insights on how to create more meaningful connections through physical and digital products.

Paula Zuccotti is a designer, ethnographer, and author. She is the founder of The Overworld, a design consultancy that helps companies understand human behavior and create better products and services. Paula is the author of "Everything We Touch," a book that explores the objects that shape our daily lives.
